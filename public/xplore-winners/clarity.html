Bridging the Career Gap with AI-Driven Matching and Fact-Based Talent Development

LONDON

Jathub CIC and University College London (UCL) are pleased to announce that Clarity, a pioneering career development platform, has been selected as a winner of the Jathub X-plore Ideathon. By utilizing data-driven insights and AI-powered matching, Clarity addresses the critical disconnect between young professionals seeking experience and companies searching for qualified talent.

Solving the "Missing Experience" Paradox

The most common barrier for students entering the workforce is the "missing experience" rejection. Clarity identifies that both businesses and students face significant hurdles:

  • For Companies (B2B): Traditional recruiting is often inefficient and time-intensive, with many organizations lacking visibility among young talent and relying on intuition rather than fact-based metrics.
  • For Students (B2C): Students often struggle to find relevant opportunities among fragmented platforms, frequently lacking insight into what specific experiences are necessary for their professional growth.

A Data-Driven Matching Ecosystem

Clarity transforms these challenges into a streamlined, four-step hiring process that prioritizes trust and efficiency. The platform's solution—"The Hidden Career Gap"—leverages a sophisticated technical engine to create meaningful connections:

  • Assessment Engine: Provides comprehensive insights into personality, team fit, and performance.
  • Fact-Based Certification: Replaces subjective self-marketing with verified skills and program experience, fostering reliability in candidate profiles.
  • AI-Powered Matching: An advanced algorithm maps career paths and opportunities to deliver tailored matches, ensuring that neither applicant nor employer requires prior awareness of each other to connect.

Vision for the Future of Recruitment

The project, led by a professional team including Co-Founders Pascal Altendorf and Yesieniia Liaskina, alongside a specialized group of data and AI experts, aims to become the premier career navigation tool for students. Clarity’s vision is to act as the primary infrastructure connecting talent development with corporate hiring, ultimately transforming fragmented decision-making into a cohesive, data-driven system.

"Clarity has demonstrated exceptional innovation in addressing the friction points of early-career hiring," noted the Ideathon organizing committee. "By providing young professionals with clarity regarding their potential and equipping companies with reliable, data-backed candidate matching, this platform is setting a new standard for the recruitment industry."

With a successful MVP, established pilot customers, and a robust matching logic already in place, Clarity is now focused on integrating pilot feedback and broadening its industry portfolio to scale its impact.
